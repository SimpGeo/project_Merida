Restaurant Introduction

Bring the finest and freshest foods to our customers, is our first priority in Treasure Pot.

Feeling hungry and having difficulty of choosing a restaurant? Try Treasure Pot! Our menu provides more than 30 different kinds of food, fish, beef, lamb, chicken, shrimp, and a bunch of other home special cuisines, and they are all you can eat!

Food quality is the least thing you need to worry about in Treasure Pot. In order to deliver the best taste to our customers, all of our foods are ordered from our exclusive factory. And to make sure of the freshness, every unused ingredient which lasts two days will be replaced by new ingredients.

What are you waiting for? Come and taste!
